Get the ratio of explicit measures to implicit measures for this Drawing as a whole.

This is the ratio of the explicit dimension of the Drawing to the implicit dimension of its contents.

This method is different from getExplicitHeightPerImplicitHeight() and getExplicitWidthPerImplicitWidth() in that it takes into account both height constraints and width constraints.

Returns
The ratio of explicit measures to implicit measures for this diagram as a whole.
